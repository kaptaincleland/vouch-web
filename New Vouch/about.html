Who We Are

Our story is what drives our mission and vision for Vouch. We are an organization founded by Ghanaians who have personally benefited from being able to attain higher education and want to help ensure the younger generation receives similar opportunities. With many High School graduates not been able to attend post-secondary school due to the high costs and financial burdens at home. Opportunities for higher education in technology often only go towards already privileged students. Vouch Initiative International provides an avenue for technological training and certifications for young people who would otherwise be unable to pursue this career path. Founded in 2020, our core is to see the people we serve become problem solvers.

Guided by Christian principles, Vouch also prioritizes teaching students not only practical skills but Core Values that will help guide them as they grow as leaders in their community. Those principles of Teamwork, Transparency, Leadership, Dedication, Discipline & Integrity provides the threshold for our organization. This allows for Vouch to not only impact its students but the larger community as well.

We see a responsibility to provide people with the information, tutoring and guidance needed to help develop these technological skills. Through these efforts, Vouch will implement its mission to empower youth to be agents of change in I.T. innovation and fulfill its vision of boosting the ingenuity of youth through I.T. education to help develop the community.

Our goal is to raise up more leaders with an ownership in Vouch’s mission.
